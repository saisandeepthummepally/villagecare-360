01

Introduction

Healthcare in India is not equally distributed. Cities have specialists; villages have the wait.

  • ⚖️

    Urban–rural inequality

    A widening gap in access to quality healthcare between cities and villages.

  • 🩺

    Shortage of specialists

    Rural regions lack the specialist doctors that complex conditions demand.

  • 🚌

    Travel cost & delay

    Patients travel far and wait long — costing money, time, and sometimes lives.

  • 📲

    Need for digital health

    A scalable, affordable digital healthcare solution is no longer optional.

02

Problem Statement

The cracks in rural healthcare, made visible.

01

No rural specialists

Villages lack access to specialized doctors for anything beyond primary care.

02

Limited diagnostics

Diagnostic facilities are scarce, delaying accurate detection of disease.

03

High mortality

Elevated maternal & infant mortality rates driven by gaps in timely care.

04

Delayed emergencies

Critical emergency care arrives late when every minute counts.

What VillageCare 360 sets out to achieve.

Reduce travel burden

What is Telemedicine?

Remote diagnosis and treatment, powered by technology.

🔬

Remote diagnosis & treatment

Care delivered using technology, across any distance.

🎥

Video consultation

Face-to-face appointments with doctors from anywhere.

📡

Remote monitoring

Vital signs tracked continuously from the patient's home.

📝

Digital prescriptions

Prescriptions issued and delivered electronically.

07

Working Process

From registration to recovery — step by step.

  1. Step 1

    Patient registers

    At the rural center or through the mobile app.

  2. Step 2

    Symptoms entered

    Details are captured into the system.

  3. Step 3

    AI suggests diagnosis

    The system proposes possible conditions.

  4. Step 4

    Urban doctor connects

    A specialist joins via secure video call.

  5. Step 5

    Prescription sent

    The Rx is delivered digitally to the patient.

  6. Step 6

    Follow-up monitoring

    Recovery is tracked remotely over time.
